About Us

Welcome to your EdgeLabel Platform, built with a "local-first" philosophy for complete data privacy and control. The EdgeLabel is developed by a team dedicated to simplifying data labeling for researchers, educators, and data scientists. Our mission is to provide an intuitive, open-source tool for annotating CSV data with a focus on usability and accessibility.

EdgeLabel empowers users to annotate their datasets quickly and securely—directly in their browser. Unlike cloud-based solutions, your data never leaves your device, ensuring privacy and full control. With an intuitive interface, support for multiple annotation types (classification, bounding boxes, etc.), and automatic local storage, you can organise and label data effortlessly—no backend servers, no subscriptions, just pure productivity. Ideal for researchers, developers, and small teams who prioritise privacy and simplicity.

We value user privacy and feedback. Learn more about our data practices in our Privacy Policy.
