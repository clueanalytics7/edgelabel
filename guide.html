Getting Started

Welcome to the EdgeLabel Platform! Follow these steps to annotate your CSV data:

  1. Upload a CSV File: Click "Upload CSV" and select a CSV with text columns (e.g., "Text,Description").
  2. Annotate Data: Select a label (e.g., Positive, Neutral) and click "Save Annotation" or press Enter to save and move to the next item.
  3. Navigate: Use "Previous" (←) and "Next Data" (→) buttons or arrow keys to move through items.
  4. View Annotations: Click "View Annotations" to see all saved annotations in a modal.
  5. Customize Settings: Open "Settings" to select a column, toggle light/dark theme, or add custom labels.
  6. Clear Annotations: Click "Clear Annotations" to reset annotations (download CSV first to save progress).
  7. Download Results: Click "Download CSV" to export annotations.

Tips

  • Use keyboard shortcuts: 1-5 for labels, Ctrl+Z to undo.
  • Check the privacy notice and accept/decline analytics tracking.
  • Ensure CSV files are UTF-8 encoded to avoid parsing errors.
  • Test on mobile devices for responsive design.
